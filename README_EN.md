## Hi! I'm Althea ⭐  ``Junior GameDev``  
  
I am currently a **student of VideoGame Design and Development**, although my path hasn't exactly been a straight line.
  
After graduating from high school, I completed an advanced vocational program in **Industrial Automation and Robotics**, later specializing in **Artificial Intelligence and Big Data**. 
This journey helped me discover just how much I am drawn to logic and structure—to analysis and design. It also showed me how a piece of code can get stuck in my head for days on end, 
refusing to leave even while I sleep.
  
I am confident that my previous studies have provided me with knowledge that will prove useful in the future, and I am incredibly excited to finally be able to synthesize technical and creative thinking.
  
Thank you for reading!
